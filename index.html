‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Download Tribly from the App Store and sign up with your college email address (.edu). You'll need to verify your email to start connecting with classmates.
I'm not receiving the verification email
Check your spam/junk folder first. If you still don't see it, make sure you're using a valid .edu email address and contact our support team.
How do I join a study group?
Browse study groups in the Groups section, or search for your specific class. Tap "Join" to request access to any group that interests you.
Can I use Tribly if I'm not a college student?
Tribly is designed exclusively for college students with .edu email addresses. This helps maintain our campus-focused community.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Use the report button on any post or message, or contact our support team directly. We take community safety seriously and investigate all reports promptly.
The app is crashing or won't load
Try force-closing and reopening the app. If issues persist, restart your device or contact support with your device model and iOS version.
